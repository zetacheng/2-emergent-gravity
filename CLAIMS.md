# Claim Ledger

Allowed statuses are `PROPOSED`, `SUPPORTED`, `VERIFIED`, `FAILED`, `RETIRED`,
`CONDITIONAL`, and `INCONCLUSIVE`.

`VERIFIED` requires a closed gate, reproducible artifacts, and reviewer
acceptance. `SUPPORTED` is weaker than `VERIFIED`. Failed and retired claims
must remain recorded so that scientific history is preserved.

No scientific claims were entered during infrastructure initialization.
